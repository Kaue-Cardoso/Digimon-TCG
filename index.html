What are Digital Monsters?!
Let's take a look at their world!

DATA 1: DIGITAL MONSTERS


One day, an artificially intelligent virus began to spread among the world's computers. It changed its shape and form as it traveled through the network, and evolved into the life forms known as

Digital Monsters, or Digimon!!​

Currently known primary traits

  • Born from eggs
  • Are not differentiated into male or female
  • Consume food in the Digital World as their energy source
  • May die due to external factors such as sickness and death, or old age
  • Do not carry out reproductive activity
  • Upon death, they create a new generation by laying an egg
  • Grow by changing their forms through Digivolution
  • Natural instinct gives them powerful combat abilities
  • ​The name for the digital life forms residing in the Digital World. They are the only creaturesindigenous to the Digital World, which highly resembles the real world.
  • Many kinds of Digital Monsters exist, and their numbers have increased with each year as the Digital World expands.

DATA 2: DIGIVOLUTION


Digimon are born from Digi-Eggs.​

Digimon grow when consuming programs and data, or when they triumph in battle, allowing them to Digivolve! They eventually die of old age and become Digi-Eggs again. Digimon reside in the Earth's various electronic networks as well as in the Digital World. As they are essentially computer data they can download additional data to themselves which makes them stronger.
If they download enough data, Digivolution is triggered. During Digivolution, the Digimon's appearance changes and, in most instances, becomes significantly stronger than before. The resultant form of the Digimon almost always has a different name than the previous. However, the Digimon's consciousness remains unchanged. If a Digimon does not absorb enough data needed to maintain the form it will not be able to retain that form for long and revert to a lower Level in a matter of time.

    Main Digivolution Stages (English adaptation terms with Japanese terms in brackets):
  • Fresh (幼年期 I Younenki I?, lit. "Baby I")
  • In-Training (幼年期 II Younenki II?, lit. "Baby II")
  • Rookie (成長期 Seichouki?, lit. "Child")
  • Champion (成熟期 Seijukuki?, lit. "Adult")
  • Ultimate (完全体 Kanzen-tai?, lit. "Perfect")
  • Mega (究極体 Kyuukyoku-tai?, lit. "Ultimate")

The evolutionary states of Digimon have "levels," with "Mega" at their pinnacle.

Digimon possessing the unbridled power of Mega could be a formidable weaponin the network of the real world…

DATA 3: Digital World


Digimon live in a world known as the Digital World!
The Digital World is a complex digital universe, a space that exists in a parallel dimension to ours, composed of computer data. Its topography is varied and often illogical by our standards. It can feature everything from vast forests with electronic circuit trees to sandy deserts that are, in fact, firewalls of old systems. There are floating islands, seas of data, and mountain ranges that resemble stacks of code.
The passage of time in the Digital World is not linear like ours; it can be faster or slower depending on the region and events that affect it. Gravity is also inconsistent, working in some places and not in others, which allows entire islands to float in the digital sky.​

THE DIGITAL WORLD IS NOT STATIC

It is in constant evolution. New areas emerge from discarded data or bugs, while others may disappear. It is, essentially, the physical manifestation of all the networks and information from the human world. Problems in our internet systems, for example, can cause anomalies or distortions in the fabric of the Digital World, showing the strong connection between the two worlds, even if it's indirect and imperceptible to most of us.
